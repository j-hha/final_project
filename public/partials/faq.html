What is considered a save amount of caffeine per day?
According to the FDA, it is generally considered save for healthy adults to consume up to 400 milligrams of caffeine - 4 to 5 cups of coffee - a day. Some people might be more sensitive to caffeine and experience side effects, such as nervouseness, even when consuming less caffeine. Find out more ...
What is the deal with flavored coffee based beverages?
Many flavored Lattes contain high amounts of sugar. In 2016, the UK based Charity 'Action on Sugar' analyzed 131 beverages and found:
"98% of hot flavoured drinks would receive a ‘red’ (high) label for excessive levels of sugars per serving – with 35% containing the same amount or more sugar than a can of Coca Cola"
How much sugar is healthy?
The WHO recommends that adults consume no more 25 grams - or 6 teaspoons - of free sugar per day. This limit applies to added sugar only and not to sugar naturally occurring in milk, fruit and vegetables.
